## <span style="color:rgb(129,15,50)"><font face="verdana">**Instructions**</font></span>

*English version*

**BULGUBULGU** is a simple arcade acion platformer game based on a commonly known game named Bubble Bobble.

* Getting started

To start the game, run ./bulgubulgu.py in a console. 

* How to play?

In the game there are three levels, each limited to a single screen, with no scrolling. You - as a player - control an alien using following keys:

* LEFT - move left
* RIGHT - move right
* UP - jump
* SPACE - shoot

The alien under your control can move along platforms, fall to lower ones, jump to upper ones and over gaps. Each level has a particular number of enemies represented by ghosts that you have to defeat in order to move to the next level. If a screen has gaps on top and bottom edges, both player and ghosts can fall through bottom gaps and appear at the top ones. You can trap the enemies by shooting bubbles (which are blue) towards them. If your bubble reach a ghost before it starts to float, it turns into a red bubble and by colliding with it you can kill the enemy. But remember to hurry up - ghosts will not be trapped forever! And the faster you kill the red bubble, the more points you get - the current score is being displayed at the top right corner of the screen. After defeating an enemy, a gem item may appear somewhere on the screen - pick it up to get extra points. Starting the game, you have three lifes - your current number of lifes is being displayed at the top left corner of the screen. Every time you collide with an enemy that is not trapped in a bubble you lose one of the lifes and some points. And for some time after that you are protected - which means you are allowed to bump into ghosts without any consequences.
